## Lead Qualification Bot

Voiceflow chatbot that qualifies leads, books meetings, and syncs with CRM. This automated solution achieved a 67% qualification rate, significantly improving the sales pipeline efficiency.

### Key Features

- Intelligent lead qualification conversations
- Automated meeting scheduling
- CRM integration and data synchronization
- Natural language processing for better user experience

### Results

The bot has successfully qualified hundreds of leads while reducing manual work for the sales team, allowing them to focus on high-value conversations with pre-qualified prospects.
