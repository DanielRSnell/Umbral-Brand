## Customer Onboarding Flow

N8N workflow connecting Stripe, email, Slack, and CRM. Reduced onboarding time by 75%.

### Automation Stack

This comprehensive automation workflow orchestrates the entire customer onboarding process, connecting multiple platforms:

- **Stripe**: Payment processing and subscription management
- **Email**: Automated welcome sequences and documentation
- **Slack**: Team notifications and collaboration
- **CRM**: Customer data synchronization and tracking

### Impact

By automating the onboarding process, we reduced the time required to onboard new customers by 75%, while simultaneously improving the customer experience and reducing manual errors.

### Workflow Design

The N8N workflow handles everything from initial payment confirmation through account setup, credential delivery, and team notification, ensuring no steps are missed and all stakeholders are informed.
